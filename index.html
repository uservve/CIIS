About Our School

Curriculum Innovation International School is one of the best international schools in Riyadh and a Cognia accredited school that provides an interactive environment for little minds to grow. Our academic planning is based on the American criteria of Pearson Curriculum. Arabic, Islamic Studies and Saudi identity are a basic part of our educational process.
